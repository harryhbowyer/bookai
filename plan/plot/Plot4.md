# Plot 4: First Clash with Krug's Horde

The group ambushes a raiding party led by villain Bloodhowl Krug, the berserker warlord who has embraced the corruption; Garrick's berserker rage clashes brutally with Krug's hammers in a violent melee, allowing Thorne's explosive gadgets to scatter the minions, but Krug escapes with a clue to the perpetual flame core.
