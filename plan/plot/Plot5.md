# Plot 5: Mirael's Sabotage

Dwarf saboteur Forgecurse Mirael, posing as an ally from Deepholm, infiltrates the camp and poisons their supplies with corrosive elixirs, forcing Lirien's paranoia to flare as she uncovers the betrayal; the group survives thanks to Sylara's herbal countermeasures, but Mirael steals a map leading to Lirien's amulet, sowing distrust.
